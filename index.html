Frequently Asked Questions

A Full Stack Developer Bootcamp is an intensive training program that teaches both front-end and back-end web development. It covers programming languages, frameworks, databases, and deployment processes to prepare students for a career in web development.

No prior experience is required! Our bootcamp is designed for beginners as well as those looking to enhance their coding skills. However, familiarity with basic programming concepts can be beneficial.

The duration varies depending on the format. Full-time bootcamps typically last 12–16 weeks, while part-time options may take 6–9 months.
